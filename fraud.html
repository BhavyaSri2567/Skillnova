💡 How to Stay Safe on SkillNova

🔍

Check Trust Score

Always work with employers who have a Trust Score above 70. Low scores mean fewer verified reviews.

💰

Avoid Unrealistic Pay

If a job offers ₹5,000+/day for simple work, it's likely a scam. Normal rates are ₹400–₹1,500/day.

💬

Chat First, Then Meet

Always discuss work details through SkillNova chat before meeting anyone in person.

📍

Verify Location

Be cautious of jobs with no specific location or jobs that ask you to travel far without advance payment.

🚫

Never Share OTP

SkillNova will never ask for your OTP, Aadhaar, or bank details through chat or phone calls.

🛡️

Report Suspicious Jobs

See something suspicious? Use the fraud check tool or tap "Report" on any job listing.
