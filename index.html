How It Works

From Sign Up to Getting Paid
in 5 Simple Steps

SkillNova makes it easy for workers and employers to connect, verify, and complete work — all in one place.

📝
01

Register & Create Profile

Sign up as Worker or Employer. Add your skills, location, pricing, and availability.

🤖
02

AI Verification & Trust Score

Our AI checks your profile, verifies image authenticity, and assigns you a Trust Score.

🔍
03

Smart Job Matching

AI matches you with nearby jobs based on your skills, location, and trust score.

💬
04

Chat & Accept Work

Chat directly with employers, agree on pricing, accept the job and start working.

⭐
05

Get Rated & Build Portfolio

Complete the job, get rated, and watch your Trust Score and portfolio grow.

About SkillNova

A Unified Platform for
Decent Work & Economic Growth

SkillNova was built as part of a Community Service Project (CSP) to address the lack of employment opportunities and skill access for workers in rural and semi-urban areas of India. By leveraging AI technology and digital connectivity, we aim to bridge the gap between skilled workers and employers — ensuring fair wages, transparent pricing, and inclusive economic growth for all.
