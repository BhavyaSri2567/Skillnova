⚙️ How NLP Works in SkillNova

1

Language Detection

When you type, our system automatically detects whether you're writing in Telugu or English using Unicode character analysis. Telugu characters fall in the range U+0C00–U+0C7F.

2

Keyword Translation

Telugu job-related keywords are instantly mapped to English equivalents. For example, "ప్లంబర్" → "plumber", "ఎలక్ట్రీషియన్" → "electrician".

3

AI Processing

The translated query is sent to Groq AI with instructions to respond in the user's language. The AI understands context and gives relevant job/skill advice.

4

Response in Native Language

The AI responds in Telugu or English based on what you typed. Rural workers who aren't comfortable with English can use SkillNova fully in Telugu!
